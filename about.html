About ToolGhor

The privacy-first utility suite for the modern web.

Our Mission

ToolGhor was built on a simple premise: Your files should never leave your device. In an era where data privacy is increasingly fragile, we provide a suite of powerful tools that operate entirely within your browser's local environment.

Privacy First

Traditional "online" tools upload your PDFs, images, and videos to their servers. ToolGhor uses WebAssembly and modern Web APIs to process everything locally. No uploads. No leaks.

No Barriers

We believe in tools that just work. No accounts, no subscriptions, and no tracking. Just open the page, add your files, and get the job done instantly.

How It Works

ToolGhor leverages the latest in browser technology to bring desktop-class performance to the web:

WebAssembly (WASM)

We use FFmpeg compiled to WASM to handle complex video conversion and trimming directly in your browser's memory.

Local PDF Engine

Using pdf-lib and pdf.js, we can manipulate, merge, and render PDF documents without any server-side processing.

On-Device OCR

Text extraction is powered by Tesseract.js, which runs optical character recognition locally, ensuring your documents remain private.

Tech Stack

ToolGhor is built with transparency and performance in mind:

Vanilla JavaScript WebAssembly pdf-lib Tesseract.js FFmpeg.wasm JSZip
